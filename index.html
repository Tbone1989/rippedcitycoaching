MY TRANSFORMATION STORY

March 2019 - Rock Bottom

Losing my mom shattered me. Food became my escape, the gym became a stranger. I gained weight rapidly and lost all motivation to care about my health.

The Breaking Point

One day I looked in the mirror and couldn't recognize myself. I realized my mom wouldn't want me destroying my life. That moment changed everything.

July 2020 - Transformed

97 pounds lighter, mentally stronger, and with a new purpose: helping others breakthrough the same struggles I faced. Your transformation starts with one decision.

What I Learned

  • Consistency beats perfection every time
  • Your mind is your biggest muscle
  • Every day is a new chance to start
  • Your struggles can become your strength
