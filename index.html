RIPPED CITY

From Rock Bottom to Transformation

After losing my mother, I hit rock bottom at 338 pounds. Every blood test came back with concerning results. I was physically exhausted, emotionally drained, and medically at risk.

That's when everything changed. With guidance from my friend Mark Alvisi and training partner Duveuil Valcena, I began the most transformative journey of my life.

97 pounds lost in 16 months (March 2019 - July 2020)

This transformation wasn't just physical. With every pound shed, I gained mental clarity, emotional strength, and a sense of purpose I'd never experienced before.

Ripped City Inc was born from this journey - not just as a clothing brand, but as a testament to what's possible when you refuse to give up on yourself.
